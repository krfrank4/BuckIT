Understanding Budgeting

Budgeting is a critical aspect of financial literacy. It involves creating a plan to spend your money wisely and ensuring that your expenses do not exceed your income. By budgeting effectively, you can save money, reduce debt, and achieve your financial goals.

Investing for the Future

Investing is another essential component of financial literacy. It involves putting your money into assets such as stocks, bonds, and real estate to generate returns over time. By investing wisely, you can grow your wealth and secure your financial future.
